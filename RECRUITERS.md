# RECRUITERS.md — Contract/Fractional Recruitment Agencies (living doc)

Purpose: track recruitment agencies and fractional-executive platforms
worth registering with as a candidate — researched 2026-07-17 ahead of
planned registration next sprint. Complements the direct-outreach CRM
(`FACTS.md` Business Development System) — this is the "represented by an
agency" channel rather than direct warm/networking outreach.

## Context this list assumes
- Garden leave until March 2027: contracting/consulting allowed, full-time
  **employment** is not — interim/fractional engagements (even full-time
  hours) are fine, just not employee status.
- Day rate target: ¥100,000-150,000/day.
- Has both a Japanese company and a UK company ready to invoice.
- No HR-tech/job-board/recruitment-platform **clients** before April 2027
  — this constraint is about who pays for the work, not about which
  agencies can represent him, so registering with any of these is fine.

  defunct/too obscure to pursue.

## Gap identified
No Japan-specific "fractional CPO network" or interim-management
association turned up beyond Japan Interim Management K.K. (not a
marketplace, no open candidate pipeline). Japan's fractional-*product*-
leadership infrastructure looks thin — mostly runs through personal
network and the global platforms above, not local agencies. This
reinforces the value of the direct warm/networking outreach system
already built (`OUTREACH.md`) rather than relying on agencies alone.
